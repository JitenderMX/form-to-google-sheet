FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. Why should I choose “MPS” as my dream destination?

Education is a journey that instills the best of human values, imparting competence, capabilities, and a vision to empower and face the challenges of the world. Here at “MPS,” our aim is not only to produce the best professionals for society but also sound entrepreneurs who can contribute to making this country a developed one.

2. How can I clear my doubts in case of any query?

In case of any query regarding the admission procedure, you can contact the student advisor on the college premises either personally or telephonically. If you have doubts regarding the academics and future prospects of any stream, you can contact the academic coordinator. You can also send your queries via email round the clock.
If you have any requests or suggestions regarding matters related to the college, you can send them via email. Your suggestions will be of great value to us.

3. What kind of placement support does the college provide?

We have close contacts in the industry to arrange for suitable and timely placements in multinational as well as Indian companies. For this purpose, MPS has established a “Corporate Resource Centre” at its head office in New Delhi, with a team of professionals working for the future of our students. Apart from a focused study pattern, students will be provided with proper placement training, which includes various types of tests and preparation through “Mock GDs and Interviews” to make them conversant in every aspect.

4. What are the eligibility criteria for applying to MPS?

The candidate must hold a 10+2 degree with a minimum of 50% marks from any board or university for bachelor’s degree courses. For master’s courses, the candidate must hold a bachelor’s degree with at least 50% marks or an equivalent CGPA from any university.
For Biotechnology and Hotel Management, a Common Entrance Test (CET) will be conducted. Dates for the CET can be confirmed from the college office in the month of March.
For the Industry Integrated MBA, a separate entrance test will be conducted. Candidates shortlisted on the basis of test performance will be called for a group discussion followed by a personal interview.
For bachelor’s degree courses (BBA & BCA), candidates will only be called for a group discussion and interview, and on the basis of this, admission will be confirmed. The names of the selected candidates will also appear on the Institute’s website around the same time.

5. What is the mode of payment of fees? Is there any provision for scholarships/concessions?

The fee can be paid either by cheque (local), demand draft, or cash.
It is the endeavor of MPS that no student be denied the opportunity to pursue studies for want of adequate financial resources. Substantial need-based scholarships are available for students belonging to economically disadvantaged families. The institute also assists students interested in acquiring educational loans from financial institutions, subject to meeting the stipulated conditions. In addition, the institute awards merit scholarships and a 10% waiver in tuition fees to students belonging to SC/ST categories.
